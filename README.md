## ⚙️ System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run the software smoothly:

- Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- At least 8GB of RAM.
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
- A stable internet connection for the chat assistant features.

If your system uses a dedicated graphics card, the software performs better when drawing complex 3D shapes. Ensure your drivers stay up to date for the best performance.

## 🚀 How to use your new assistant

After you launch the application, you see a chat window on the right side of the screen. This is where you interact with the AI.

1. Open a new project or an existing 3D model.
2. Click the chat button in the top menu if the panel is hidden.
3. Type a command into the text box. For example, you might type "Create a cube with a side length of 50 millimeters."
4. Press the Enter key to send your request.
5. Watch the software generate the geometry in your viewport.

The AI understands common design terms. If you want to change a shape, simply type "Change the height to 70 millimeters" or "Add a hole to the center of the block." The system updates the model instantly within your browser.

## 🧠 Connecting your intelligence model

The AI assistant relies on a language model to understand your typing. By default, the software connects to secure services that process your commands.

To configure your specific settings:

1. Open the "Settings" menu inside the application.
2. Select the "AI Provider" tab.
3. Enter your unique API key from your selected provider. 
4. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

The tool works best when you keep your settings organized. If the chat stops responding, check your internet connection and verify that your API key remains active on your provider account page.

## 🛠️ Common tasks

You can perform many complex actions with simple phrases. Here are a few examples to help you start designs:

- "Create a cylinder with a radius of 20."
- "Rotate the object 90 degrees on the X axis."
- "Move the selected item 10 units to the left."
- "Delete the active layer."

The software handles the math. It calculates the coordinates and draws the lines exactly where you request. You spend less time using the mouse for manual clicks and more time creating your concepts.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting tips

If you encounter issues during installation or usage, check these common fixes:

- The software fails to open: Ensure you have administrative rights on your Windows machine to allow the installer to add the necessary files.
- The chat window stays blank: Check if your firewall blocks the connection to the AI provider. You may need to grant permission to the application in your security settings.
- The model lags during updates: Close other programs that use high memory to free up resources for the 3D engine.
- Commands do not work: Use clear, simple language. If the AI does not understand a complex sentence, break it into two smaller steps.

## 🛡️ Privacy and local execution

Your design data stays inside your browser. The AI only receives the instructions you type. It does not store your geometry files or access private folders on your computer. This architecture keeps your design work secure while you enjoy the speed of automated modeling.
